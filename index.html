About Us

Innovative Human Resource Management

IHR was established in 2018 after realising the need to modernise Human Resource Management in Zimbabwe. IHR seeks to be a leader and an active participant of the evolution of Human Resources from traditional ways to keeping up with modern trends. Our motivation comes from innovating existing Human Resources functions, introduce them to companies and give them a competitive edge on the global market. IHR seeks to be leaders and strong advocates of Artificial Intelligence in the field of human resources and beyond. our ultimate goal is to move along with the times and help our clients achieve organizational effectiveness. IHRM will use its vast experience in Human Resources to assist and advice companies in various industries and sectors to manage their human capital in modern and acceptable best practice Human resources initiatives. there is no company too big or too small for us. We value and appreciate start-ups who want advice in HR best practice. Contact us and we can help you manage your human capital to achieve your goals.
